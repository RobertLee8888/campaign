How we score KOLs.

Calls are the input. KOLs are the output. We never score a single tweet — we score the account once we have the full call history.

01 ExtractNLP identifies each call (ticker, direction, time, confidence). Sentiment posts and replies are filtered out.
02 AnchorEach call gets an entry point and a verification window from market data.
03 BacktestEqual-weight, no leverage, failed calls counted, deleted posts recovered from archive data.
04 ScoreThe full call history is aggregated into a 6-dimension account score: Score · ROI · Win Rate · Quality · Risk · Stability.
If a KOL deletes a failed call, the score does not change.
